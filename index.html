Getting started is easy

Earn Stars and get rewarded in a few easy steps.

Download the Starbucks app

Join in the app to get access to the full range of Starbucks Rewards benefits. You can also join online.

Order and pay how you'd like

Use cash, credit/debit card or save some time and pay right through the app. You'll collect Stars all ways. Learn how

Earn Stars, get Rewards

As you earn Stars, you can reddem them for Rewards-like free food, drinks, and more. Start redeeming with as little as 25 Stars!
